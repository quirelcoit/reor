CORPLAN – ICT Division 2026 Work Plan

The 2026 ICT Work Plan is designed to strengthen system stability, support operational continuity, and initiate phased modernization in a controlled and manageable manner.

ERP & Core System Modernization (Phase 1)

To begin the phased implementation of an Integrated Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system, starting with selected core operational modules.

For 2026, the focus will be on: Billing System and Collection Monitoring.

  • Initial deployment of ERP modules for Billing and Collection
  • Provisioning of a dedicated server environment for ERP operations
  • Monitoring and evaluation during the first phase of implementation
  • Phase 1: Billing & Collection modules
  • Assessment and stabilization period
  • Gradual consideration of additional modules subject to performance, operational readiness, and management direction
  • Improved data consolidation within billing and collection processes
  • Better visibility of receivables and payment tracking
  • More structured reporting capability
  • Foundational step toward long-term system integration

The ERP initiative will be implemented progressively, with adjustments as necessary based on operational feedback and system performance.

Infrastructure & Data Storage

To strengthen system reliability through preventive maintenance and structured data storage management.

  • Deployment of NAS storage for centralized backup management
  • Organized storage and safeguarding of ICT equipment
  • Reduced risk of data loss
  • Improved backup management practices

These measures are preventive in nature and are intended to minimize operational risk.

Network & Connectivity Stability

To maintain stable internal and inter-office connectivity to support operational processes.

  • Upgrade and maintenance of network switches and LAN components
  • Structured cabling and connector management
  • Support for internal display and communication systems
  • Improved internal network stability
  • More consistent inter-office connectivity
  • Better support for system-based operations

Network improvements are aimed at maintaining operational reliability and may be enhanced further as needed.

Field Operations Continuity

To maintain uninterrupted support for meter reading and billing-related field activities.

  • Maintenance of spare rugged meter reading devices
  • Spare portable printers and accessories
  • Ongoing support for meter reading software

These items serve as contingency support to prevent operational disruption in case of equipment failure.

  • Reduced risk of meter reading delays
  • Sustained billing cycle continuity
  • Operational flexibility in the field

Disaster Communication Preparedness

To strengthen communication capability during major outages or emergency situations.

  • Use of Starlink as an alternative connectivity option
  • Monitoring of connection performance
  • Coordination with management on emergency communication procedures
  • Enhanced communication capability during network disruptions
  • Additional layer of operational preparedness

Important Clarification: This initiative provides communication redundancy and risk mitigation but does not eliminate all external connectivity risks.

ICT Capability Development

To gradually enhance internal ICT technical capability and monitoring support.

  • Utilization of development tools to support internal system improvements
  • Improved responsiveness to system issues
  • Gradual reduction in reliance on external service providers
  • Strengthened internal technical capacity

The ICT Division remains committed to supporting stable, reliable, and responsible technology operations for QUIRELCO.
